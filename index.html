Site Design & Development

We're Blunt, an intentionally small, independent team of designers and developers based in Toronto. Agencies and small businesses come to us to help them design, develop, and maintain their sites.

We have fun, keep things simple, and try new things. We listen carefully to our clients to craft world-class sites that stand out and people love to use.
